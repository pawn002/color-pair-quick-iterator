Notes

LCH Limits: Shows variants of your color within the sRGB gamut. The palette reveals how hue constrains your lightness and chroma options — for example, strong yellows are only possible at high lightness.

WCAG 2: This score is for reference only—do not use this score for judging inclusive contrast.

Constant chroma: Constrains sliders to tones that preserve your input color's chroma and hue. Disable to access a wider tonal range, though some generated tones may clash with your original color. When disabled, the Show Gradient option ranges from black to white.

Show Gradient: This option renders a tone gradient behind the slider inputs. The gradient shows the tonal possibilities of your inputted color.
